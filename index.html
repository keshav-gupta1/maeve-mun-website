The Conference

Built like a diplomatic summit, run for student leaders.

Maeve MUN brings together delegates from schools and universities for researched diplomacy, moderated caucuses, crisis simulations, policy drafting, and social events in the heart of Meerut.

The experience is designed to feel premium but purposeful: high standards, clean logistics, thoughtful committee direction, and a conference culture that rewards both preparation and courage.

FAQ

Quick answers before you register.

Who can participate?

Students from schools, colleges, and independent delegations can apply. First-time delegates are welcome.

Will accommodation be provided?

Accommodation assistance can be added after final venue confirmation. Delegates will receive options by email.

How are committees allotted?

Allotments are based on application quality, experience, preference order, and seat availability.
